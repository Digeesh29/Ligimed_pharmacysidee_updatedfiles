The Operating System for Modern Pharmacies

LigiMed is the B2B digital marketplace empowering local pharmacies. Streamline procurement, unlock financial flexibility, and compete smarter with our end-to-end supply chain solution.

The Challenge We Solve

Local pharmacies face fragmented supply chains, limited credit access, and complex procurement processes that drain time and resources.

Fragmented Supply Chain

Multiple suppliers, inconsistent pricing, and inefficient ordering processes waste valuable time.

Limited Credit Access

Traditional financing options are scarce, restricting inventory management and growth potential.

Lack of Insights

No visibility into purchasing trends, inventory optimization, or competitive pricing strategies.

Our Features

A comprehensive platform that digitizes procurement, provides embedded financing, and delivers actionable analytics.

Digital Marketplace

Browse products from verified suppliers, compare prices, and place orders in minutes.

Embedded Finance

Access flexible credit lines and payment terms tailored to your pharmacy's needs.

Inventory Management

Track stock levels, set reorder alerts, and optimize inventory with smart recommendations.

Business Analytics

Gain insights into spending patterns, profit margins, and market trends to make informed decisions.
